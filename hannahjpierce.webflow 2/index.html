My Bio:

My name is Hannah Pierce. I grew up in Burbank, WA with my parents and younger sister, Abby. I graduated from Columbia High School in 2011. I attended Columbia Basin College where I played volleyball my sophomore year. I graduated with my AA with emphasis in Cultural Anthropology in 2012.
I transferred to EWU the following winter season. I now live in Cheney where I go to school full time and work part time at the EWU Art Department as a figure model. I enjoy listening to music - I love Dave Matthews Band! I love living in the Pacific Northwest and hope to some day move to Seattle.
